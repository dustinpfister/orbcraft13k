This is OrbCraft 13k, an idea for a game that I have been thinking about for a long time but have never 
gotten around to developing it. It is inspired by the gemcraft series of games by game in a bottle, to which 
I have lost more hours of my life then I would like to mention.

How to play:

* Use the mouse to make an Orb of type Fire, Earth, Water, or Air.
* The Orb will apear in your inventory, click and drag it to a building in the game board.
* The building can now defend itself from enemy's.
* Making Orbs cost Mana.
* You make more Mana over time, you also gain it from enemy's
* Not spending Mana causes your Mana level to go up, resulting in more Mana over time, and a bonus to kills.
* You can combine Orbs to make them more powerful, and to have interesting properties.
* When you kill all the enemy waves, a new Level will start in which the enemy's will become more difficult.
* Good luck


Orbs:

Orbs come in the following types, and have the corresponding attribute bonuses

* Fire - bonus to attack
* Earth - bonus to range
* Water - bonus to health
* Air - bonus to speed

Pure Orbs, and Composite Orbs.

Making an Orb of a specific type, and combining it with only Orbs that are composed of that same type, will 
result in a pure Orb. This Pure Orb will be very powerful with it's bonus attribute. For example if you have 
a Pure Fire Orb it will have a 100% bonus to attack. As such it's attack will be higher then any other Orb 
of the same value. Making a Composite Orb on the other Hand will have a more interesting set of properties 
that depend on the Orbs used. So if you make an Orb in which you combine Fire, Earth, and Water Orbs in 
a 2,2,1 ratio. Then you will have an Orb with a 40% bonus to attack, 40% bonus to range, and a 20% 
bonus to Health.

Hit points, and Auto-healing:

The base hit point value of a building is 100. When you place an Orb into a building it's Health Value 
is added to that base value. In addition the orb will auto-heal the building at a rate of 1/10 the orbs 
Health Value. Building Pure Water Orbs will help maximise this effect.
